## Prompt Modification Tips

### Adjusting Duration
- Add "slow motion" for longer scenes
- Add "time-lapse" or "sped up" for faster pacing
- Specify exact duration: "10-second shot"

### Changing Perspective
- "POV shot from user perspective"
- "Over-the-shoulder view"
- "Bird's eye view"
- "Wide establishing shot"

### Modifying Mood
- "Energetic and dynamic"
- "Calm and professional"
- "Exciting and fast-paced"
- "Elegant and sophisticated"

### Adjusting Color
- Emphasize brand colors: "dominant purple and blue tones"
- Change lighting: "warm golden hour lighting"
- Modify saturation: "vibrant saturated colors" or "muted tones"

### Technical Adjustments
- Frame rate: "24fps for cinematic" or "60fps for smooth motion"
- Camera movement: "static shot" vs "dynamic camera movement"
- Depth of field: "deep focus" vs "shallow depth of field"

## Generation Best Practices

### 1. Generate Multiple Takes
- Create 3-5 variations per scene
- Select best footage in post-production
- Mix and match best moments

### 2. Consistency Checks
- Ensure color palette matches across scenes
- Maintain visual style throughout video
- Check brand color accuracy

### 3. Quality Review
- Review at full resolution (4K when specified)
- Check for artifacts or glitches
- Ensure smooth motion and transitions

### 4. Scene Matching
- Generate related scenes in same session
- Keep lighting and style consistent
- Maintain camera movement rhythm

### 5. Iteration Strategy
- Start with provided prompts as-is
- Make small adjustments if needed
- Document changes for future reference

## Prompt Troubleshooting

### Issue: Colors not matching brand
**Solution:** Add "exact purple (#9333EA) and blue (#3B82F6) colors"

### Issue: Too much motion/chaos
**Solution:** Add "slow, smooth camera movement, minimal motion"

### Issue: Not professional enough
**Solution:** Emphasize "corporate professional aesthetic, clean minimal design"

### Issue: Wrong aspect ratio
**Solution:** Explicitly state "16:9 landscape format" or "9:16 vertical format"

### Issue: Scene too short/long
**Solution:** Add specific duration "15-second continuous shot"
